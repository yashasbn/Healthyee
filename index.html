Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. It encompasses various aspects of an individual's life, including physical fitness, mental stability, emotional resilience, and social interactions. Maintaining good health involves adopting a balanced lifestyle that includes regular exercise, proper nutrition, sufficient sleep, and effective stress management.
