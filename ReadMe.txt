## Introduction

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being. HealthyEEE aims to provide valuable information and resources to help you achieve a balanced and healthy lifestyle. Explore various aspects of health, from mental well-being to nutrition, fitness, sleep, and more.

## Features

- Learn about mental health and well-being strategies.
- Discover the importance of a balanced diet and make nutritious food choices.
- Get insights into staying active and maintaining physical fitness.
- Explore the significance of quality sleep and sleep hygiene.
- Understand gender-specific health concerns and preventive measures.
- Gain knowledge about child and adolescent health, aging, and senior health.
- Find tips for maintaining a healthy heart and supporting the immune system.

## Usage

1. Run the application: `npm start`
2. Open your web browser and visit: `http://localhost:3000`
3. Browse through different health topics and click on "Read More" for detailed information.
